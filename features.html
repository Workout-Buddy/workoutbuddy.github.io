Finding a Partner

Workout buddy helps you find a friend in the neighbourhood by accessing your location and by using algorithms to match your body goals and qualities with another person. You might even end up forming lifelong friendships!

Progress Report

Our progress report allows you to track your progress each time you workout and hence giving you a better idea on whether you have to adopt a different workout routine to achieve your body goals or if you're absolutely on the right track!

Gym Recommendations

Why limit youself to only one gym? Our application finds the best workout sessions offered in various gyms by different coaches and recommends them to you. You might end up finding yourself doing a Zumba session in a different gym exploring a new place and new people.

Diet Plan

Our diet plan helps you to keep your calories in track. What we require from you is simply a daily input of your meals so that we can guide you on your calorie intake.

Training Program

Our training program offers guidelines to weight loss, muscles gain, high-intensity workouts, Zumba sessions for all respective ages. Moreover, personalized charts of exercices targeted for different body parts will be available for you.

Gym Membership

With the premium membership, you automatically become a member of the gym you choose to go.
